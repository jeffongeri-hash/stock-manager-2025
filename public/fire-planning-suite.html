⚠️ Sequence of Returns Risk The order of investment returns matters enormously in early retirement. A -30% year in your first 3 years of retirement is far more damaging than the same loss at year 15. Consider holding 1–2 years of expenses in cash or short-term bonds as a buffer to avoid selling equities in downturns.

Early retirement bridge strategy

If you retire at 50, you'll have a gap of 12–20 years before Social Security kicks in. Plan to draw more from your portfolio in those years, then reduce withdrawals when SS begins.

Break-even age for delaying to 70

Delaying from 62 to 70 typically breaks even around age 80–82. If you expect to live past 82, delaying to 70 is almost always the optimal strategy from a lifetime benefit perspective.
